Acne

Acne is a common skin condition that occurs when hair follicles are clogged with oil and dead skin cells...

Common treatments include topical creams, antibiotics, and lifestyle changes.

Acne Scars

Acne scars are marks or areas of discolored skin left behind after an acne breakout has healed...

Treatment options include laser therapy, chemical peels, and microneedling.

Hyperpigmentation

Hyperpigmentation is the darkening of the skin due to an excess of melanin, the pigment that gives skin its color...

Treatments may include topical agents, chemical peels, and laser therapy.

Oily Skin

Oily skin is characterized by an excess production of sebum, the skin's natural oil...

Effective skincare for oily skin includes oil-free products, regular cleansing, and exfoliation.

Dry Skin

Dry skin lacks the necessary moisture and may feel tight, rough, or flaky...

Moisturizing regularly, avoiding hot showers, and using gentle cleansers are essential for managing dry skin.

Rosacea

Rosacea is a chronic skin condition that causes redness, visible blood vessels, and sometimes small, red, pus-filled bumps on the face...

Management strategies include avoiding triggers, gentle skincare, and prescription medications.
