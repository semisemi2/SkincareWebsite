Normal Skin

Normal skin is well-balanced, not too oily and not too dry. It has a smooth texture and a healthy complexion. People with normal skin often experience minimal sensitivity to skincare products.

Oily Skin

Oily skin tends to produce excess sebum, making the skin appear shiny and often leading to enlarged pores. Individuals with oily skin may be more prone to acne and blackheads.

Dry Skin

Dry skin lacks moisture, leading to a tight or rough feeling. It may appear flaky or irritated. People with dry skin may need to use hydrating products to maintain moisture levels.

Combination Skin

Combination skin features a mix of different skin types on different areas of the face. For example, the T-zone (forehead, nose, and chin) may be oily, while the cheeks are normal or dry.
